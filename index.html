Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our bottles and services

We use three primary materials: PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) for crystal-clear water bottles, PP (Polypropylene) for heat-resistant containers, and PC (Polycarbonate) for durable, reusable bottles. Each material is FDA-approved and meets international safety standards.

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive branding solutions including custom colors, logos, shapes, and sizes. Our design team will work with you to create bottles that perfectly represent your brand while maintaining optimal functionality.

With our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, we can produce up to 100,000 bottles per day. Our minimum order quantity is 1,000 units for standard bottles and 5,000 units for custom designs. We handle both small-batch and large-scale production runs.

We hold ISO 9001:2015 certification for quality management, FDA approval for food-grade materials, and BRC packaging certification. Our facility undergoes regular audits, and each production batch is tested for quality and safety compliance.

We offer worldwide shipping with reliable tracking. Standard delivery times are 2-3 weeks for regular orders and 3-4 weeks for custom orders. We can also accommodate rush orders based on availability and provide bulk shipping discounts.

Our bottles are designed with sustainability in mind. We use recycled materials when possible, optimize wall thickness to reduce plastic usage, and ensure all our products are 100% recyclable. We also offer eco-friendly packaging options and participate in plastic recycling initiatives.
